About Us

The educational complex for 50 students aged 14-18 was built with the funding of the Embassy of Japan.The center has 2 comfortable classrooms, equipped with the necessary equipment.Before being admitted, everyone took a test, and the best ones went to study.The courses are for 2 years, after graduation everyone will receive certificates.
